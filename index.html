Hello, there!
My name is Saachi Kudtarkar and I'm currently a third year mathematics of computation major at UCLA. I'm most in my element when I'm watching a TED talk, eating Veggie Grill, or having an existential crisis (sometimes even all at the same time!). Though my classes and professional experiences require I focus on math, stats, and coding, I'm still very drawn to music, writing, and the arts, as they allow me to express myself and document my experiences in a truly unique way. This website was thus born out of a desire to both put myself out there and hold myself accountable to the goal of keeping this part of me alive. Hope you enjoy!
